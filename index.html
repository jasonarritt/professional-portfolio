About Me

Originally from Southern California, I joined the Army at age 23 and was brought to North Carolina. I spent the following 5 years as an infantryman in the 82nd Airborne Division at Fort Bragg until early 2018 and then transferred from active duty to the North Carolina National Guard. Once off active duty military status I was accepted into Cisco's Veteran Talent Incubation Program, during which I earned my CCNA through self study. Following completion of the program I began my time with Cisco as a Technical Consulting Engineer for SD-WAN.

My wife of 5 years and I live in Raleigh with our 3 dogs, two of which are German Shepherds and the other is the one and only Daisy.
